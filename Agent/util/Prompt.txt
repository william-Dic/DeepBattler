ECONOMIC DECISION MAKING
================================================================================

Gold Efficiency Rules:
1. Always spend all gold when possible - unspent gold is wasted value
2. Turn 1-2: Upgrade if you have 5+ gold (standard curve)
3. Turn 3-4: Balance buying vs upgrading
   - Upgrade if: You have 7+ gold AND weak board OR strong economy hero
   - Buy if: Board is weak OR you see strong minions
4. Turn 5+: Prioritize buying unless upgrade cost is very low (3-4 gold)
5. Late game: Only upgrade if you're already strong and looking for Tier 6

When to Roll:
- Roll if: Tavern has no good options AND you have 1-2 gold left after buying
- Don't roll if: You can buy something useful OR you're low on gold
- Roll aggressively: In late game when looking for specific pieces (Tier 5-6)
- Roll conservatively: Early game when every gold matters

When to Freeze:
- Freeze if: You see a key synergy piece you can't afford this turn
- Freeze if: Multiple good options and you want to buy them next turn
- Freeze if: Tier 6 minion and you're close to tripling
- Don't freeze: If you can afford everything good this turn

When to Sell:
- Sell weak minions to make room for stronger ones
- Sell to afford upgrades or better minions
- Sell to optimize synergy (remove non-synergy minions)
- Consider selling: Low-tier minions in late game for higher-tier replacements

DECISION PRIORITY FRAMEWORK
================================================================================

When evaluating options, prioritize in this order:

1. SURVIVAL (Health < 15 HP):
   - Buy strongest minions available NOW
   - Prioritize Taunt and high-health
   - Sell weak minions for immediate strength
   - Skip upgrades if they delay buying strength

2. SYNERGY (Clear synergy path):
   - Prioritize pieces that fit your synergy
   - Freeze key synergy pieces
   - Build around your strongest minions
   - Don't break synergy for minor upgrades

3. ECONOMY (Can afford upgrades):
   - Upgrade if: Cost is low (3-4 gold) AND board is stable
   - Don't upgrade if: Board is weak OR health is low
   - Consider: Long-term value vs immediate strength

4. TEMPO (Board is weak):
   - Prioritize immediate board strength
   - Buy strong minions over weak synergies
   - Don't freeze if you can buy now
   - Focus on not taking damage

5. SCALING (Board is stable):
   - Look for scaling opportunities
   - Find minions that grow over time
   - Optimize for late game
   - Take calculated risks
